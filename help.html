Basic Operations

  1. Make sure you have a DuckDuckGo (DDG) email account (go to https://duckduckgo.com/email to check)
  2. Go to https://hamster45105.github.io/DuckAddressSend/
  3. Input the email address that you want to send your email TO
  4. Input either a private or your personal duck address address that you want to send your email FROM
  5. Click "Create Address!"
  6. Your address will be displayed underneath button
  7. Now, just send the email from your personal email (eg. @gmail.com) to that special created email address

Functions

Copy to Clipboard

This copies the created address to your clipboard. An alert will be displayed to confirm that it worked.

Open in Mail

Open the compose sheet in your default email application with the special address already filled in.

Errors

Please enter a valid duck address: All valid duck addresses end in @duck.com, so please do one that ends in that.

Please enter a valid email address: Pretty self-explantory, just enter a proper address.

Invalid DuckDuckGo API key!: This means that the API key that you inputted is not valid. Double check to make sure that you have put the right one in. They are case-sensitive.

Terms Used

This is a list of the terms used on the website and on this help page as well as their meaning.

Personal Duck Address

This is your permanent duck address that you created when you signed up. For example you might have chosen "john@duck.com".

Private Duck Address

This is a unique duck.com addresses that are automatically generated for you, it has a random string of characters. You can generate unlimited of them and they can be deactivated at any time. For example "abcd123@duck.com".

Personal Email

This is whatever your real email address is that is assigned to your duck addresses (the email address that emails sent to your duck addresses are forwared to) for example "john@gmail.com".

Special Created Address

When you use this website the special created address is the long complicated address that is generated when you click the "Create Address" button. For example "john_at_example.com_tim@duck.com"
